The AI Basics course is designed to give learners a real-world understanding of what Artificial Intelligence is and how it's already integrated into their daily lives. Without any coding, students will explore the fundamentals of AI, including how machines mimic intelligence, where AI is used (like ChatGPT, Google Maps, and facial recognition), and key concepts such as Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Computer Vision. By the end of this course, learners will confidently recognize AI technologies, use common AI tools effectively, understand basic AI terminology, and engage in informed discussions about AI ethics, bias, and its impact on jobs and society.
